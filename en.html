Discover solitudeHojšín

The most beautiful place on earth

Our Solitude can be found amongst forests, meadows and ponds. There’re only just it and nature. Its clean, modern and free concept will enchant you. A new building with details of original farmhouse offers accommodation for up to 50 people. The generous common areas, the kitchen with bar and beautiful rooms are complemented by the marvellous children’s playroom over two floors. Have breakfast on the terrace above the pond, let children run in the garden full of adventure and enjoy relaxation in nature. It takes you an hour to get to us from Prague and Budějovice, while from Brno and Pilsen it takes two. Welcome!

For your experience and comfort

Outdoor kitchen

Steaks, hamburgers or sausages. It’s just your choice what you prepare outdoors. You can serve your meal on a generous table, on the terrace above the pond or by a creek just on bread.

Real solitude

Forests, meadows, hills, streams and ponds on and around the property. And then only you and those with whom you love to spend your time. This is Hojšín and Czech Siberia.

Playground without borders

Let your children run, roll down the hill, jump, play by the creek, discover, pick flowers... And when it starts raining, take your boots off and get in. There is a playroom over two floors.

A view of the pond

Without water it is dry, without water it is boring. You can dream and drink on the terrace above the pond and don’t forget to pack rubber boots for your children - two creeks flow through the garden.

Parking directly by the house

It would be complicated and tiring to reach us on foot... A few steps away from our Solitude, you can park your car and even a bus comfortably.

Durable minimalism

Our rooms withstand something. We do not put vases on the edge of the table nor do we have stairs in the middle of the room. We have just prepared a timeless interior for you without compromises.

A new building with tradition

Siberian larch facade, floor and wall heating, burnt floor tiles, the Sonos sound system, centuries-old stone walls, a heat pump, casement windows, antiseptic vinyl floors, original beams…

They can get up at six…

… and you can still sleep. All your children can sleep together in a large room full of beds. The morning pillow battle won’t disturb you.

Jump to the pond

Right next to the terrace there is a natural bathing habitat, where you can refresh yourself after a summer trip or improve your swimming style in the evenings.

Would you like to book a whole house or just a room?

During the holidays, we only offer the rental of the entire building and the shortest possible stay is one week from Saturday to Saturday. Out of season, you can choose any single room, even for just one person for the weekend. Check out our booking calendar to find out which rooms are vacant.

Trips in the neighbourhood

QUARRY IN MILIČÍN

Beautiful flooded quarry with clear water, a few hundred metres from Miličín below the Way of the Cross and Calvary Hill. We recommend the visit, especially in summer.

MONÍNEC

A newly established ski resort with full service for both winter and summer sports. So arrive anytime.

The Region of the Knights of Blaník

Hiking and cycling trips, many monuments and places with a reference to our history – wandering through the picturesque Czech countryside under the memorable mountain of Blaník. There are many interesting places, tips for trips can be found here. Get inspired.

POST OFFICE FROM SNĚŽKA

The oldest Czech post office, which used to stand on the summit of Sněžka for years. It had to vacate a place for a new one, however, and therefore we dismantled it, beam by beam, nail by nail and transferred it to Javorová skála near Monínec. You can find it at the top station of the cable car at Monínec.

TÁBOR

Rest in urban style. Wander through the labyrinth of alleys, discover nice corners contrasting with the rawness of the town walls of the former Hussite town.

KONOPIŠTĚ

It used to be a castle, now it’s a chateau and it will not disappoint you. Get ready for a lot of fun, whether you love history, art, flowers, animals, walks in the park or a fluffy-eared bear.

GOLF IN ČERTOVO BŘEMENO, KONOPIŠTĚ, PYŠELY

Do you love golf, do you want to learn it, or just look at the perfect lawn? Choose from three golf locations or try another one each day.

TOULAVA

It is said to be a tourist area. But for us, it is a place with cottages like from a fairy tale and secret meadow paths that hide ancient Celtic stones.

CRAFT BREWERY VÍTEK

We searched and found. This combination of good beer and food, art and history invites you to a Wednesday trip.
